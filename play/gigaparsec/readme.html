* GAME DESCRIPTION *

GIGAPARSEC is a classic side-scrolling space shooter inspired by the legendary TI-99 Parsec! Pilot your spacecraft through waves of enemy fighters, dodge deadly asteroids, and collect power-ups to enhance your weapons.

How far can you travel across the galaxy? The distance is measured in parsecs - aim for a GIGAPARSEC!

>>> OBJECTIVE <<<

  • DESTROY enemy spacecraft before they destroy you
  • DODGE asteroids and enemy fire
  • COLLECT power-ups to upgrade your weapons
  • SURVIVE as long as possible and maximize your score
  • ADVANCE through increasingly difficult levels

>>> CONTROLS <<<

DESKTOP (Mouse/Keyboard):

  • MOUSE MOVEMENT - Move ship up/down
  • RIGHT CLICK or SPACE - Fire weapons
  • M KEY - Toggle background music
  • P KEY - Take screenshot
  • ESC KEY - Return to games menu

MOBILE (Touch):

  • LEFT JOYSTICK - Move ship up/down
  • FIRE BUTTON - Shoot weapons

>>> ENEMIES <<<

  • SCOUT SHIPS - Fast but weak, fly in patterns
  • FIGHTERS - Medium speed, can fire back at you
  • ASTEROIDS - Indestructible! Avoid at all costs

>>> POWER-UPS <<<

  • RAPID FIRE - Increases your firing rate temporarily
  • SHIELD - Protects you from one hit
  • EXTRA LIFE - Grants an additional ship

>>> STRATEGY TIPS <<<

  • Stay toward the left side of the screen to give yourself more reaction time for incoming threats
  • Watch for patterns in enemy formations - they often repeat
  • Prioritize dodging asteroids over shooting enemies - asteroids are indestructible!
  • Collect power-ups whenever safe - they can turn the tide in tough situations
  • Move in small, controlled motions rather than wild swings to maintain accuracy

>>> SCORING <<<

  • SCOUT SHIP: 50 points
  • FIGHTER: 100 points
  • POWER-UP COLLECTED: 25 points
  • LEVEL COMPLETION BONUS: 500 x Level
