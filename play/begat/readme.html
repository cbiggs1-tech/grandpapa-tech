* GAME DESCRIPTION *

BEGAT is a relaxing memory and trivia game tracing the genealogy of Jesus Christ from Abraham to Jesus - all 42 generations as recorded in Matthew 1:1-17.

Learn fascinating Bible trivia about each ancestor while placing them in their correct order on the timeline. Perfect for all ages!

>>> OBJECTIVE <<<

  • TAP empty timeline slots to answer trivia about each ancestor
  • IDENTIFY the correct ancestor from 4 multiple choice options
  • COMPLETE the lineage from Abraham to Jesus
  • MINIMIZE mistakes to earn bonus multipliers!

>>> HOW TO PLAY <<<

Step 1: Select a Slot

Tap on any empty slot (showing "?") on the timeline. A trivia clue will appear describing the ancestor who belongs in that position.

Step 2: Answer the Quiz

Read the Bible description and choose from 4 possible ancestors. Get it right on your first try for FULL POINTS! Wrong answers reveal the correct answer but give reduced "assisted" points.

Step 3: Redeem Mistakes

If you guess wrong, that ancestor is marked for "redemption." When you reach their proper slot later, answer correctly on the first try to earn back your lost points!

Step 4: Complete the Timeline

Fill all slots to complete the level. Your final score includes tier multipliers based on how few mistakes you made!

>>> CONTROLS <<<

DESKTOP (Mouse):

  • CLICK empty slot - Open trivia quiz for that position
  • HOVER over placed card - View approximate date range
  • MENU BUTTON (top right) - Access settings & How to Play
  • ESC KEY - Return to games menu

MOBILE (Touch):

  • TAP empty slot - Open trivia quiz for that position
  • LONG PRESS on placed card - View date range
  • CAMERA BUTTON (bottom left) - Share screenshot
  • MUSIC BUTTON (bottom right) - Toggle music

>>> GAME LEVELS <<<

  • LEVEL 1: PATRIARCHS (Easy) - Abraham to David (14 ancestors)
  • LEVEL 2: KINGS OF JUDAH (Medium) - Solomon to Jeconiah (14 ancestors)
  • LEVEL 3: POST-EXILE (Medium) - Shealtiel to Jesus (14 ancestors)
  • LEVEL 4: FULL LINEAGE (Hard) - All 42 ancestors!

>>> MENU OPTIONS <<<

  • NEW GAME - Start fresh
  • SELECT LEVEL - Choose which era to play
  • SOUND - Toggle game sound effects
  • SPEECH - Enable text-to-speech for descriptions
  • HIGH CONTRAST - Accessibility mode
  • COLORBLIND MODE - Alternative colors

>>> STRATEGY TIPS <<<

  • AIM FOR FIRST-TRY CORRECT - Full points are only awarded on first attempt!
  • REMEMBER YOUR MISTAKES - When you guess wrong, that ancestor is marked. Get it right at their proper slot to earn redemption points!
  • READ CAREFULLY - Bible verse references in clues often hint at the answer
  • KEEP MISTAKES LOW - 0-1 mistakes = 50% score bonus! Even 2-4 mistakes = 30% bonus
  • STUDY MATTHEW 1:1-17 - Learning the genealogy helps maximize your score!
  • Enable Speech mode if you prefer hearing the clues read aloud

>>> SCORING <<<

Points by Level:

  • LEVEL 1: +50 correct | +25 assisted | -20 wrong
  • LEVEL 2: +75 correct | +37 assisted | -40 wrong
  • LEVEL 3: +100 correct | +50 assisted | -70 wrong
  • LEVEL 4: +150 correct | +75 assisted | -120 wrong

Redemption System:

When you answer wrong, that ancestor is marked for redemption. Later, when you reach their correct slot, answer correctly on the first try to earn: FULL POINTS + PENALTY REFUND + MISTAKE REMOVED!

Level Completion Bonuses:

  • PERFECT (0-1 mistakes): Score x1.5 (+50%)
  • EXCELLENT (2-4 mistakes): Score x1.3 (+30%)
  • GOOD (5-8 mistakes): Score x1.15 (+15%)
  • COMPLETED (9+ mistakes): Score x1.0
  • Plus: +20% completion bonus of max possible score!
