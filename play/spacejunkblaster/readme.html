* GAME DESCRIPTION *

You are a space janitor piloting an orbital cleanup satellite! Your mission is to use your plasma torch to melt dangerous space debris before it crashes into Earth or damages the International Space Station.

The cosmos is littered with defunct satellites, rocket stages, and debris from collisions. Clean up the mess and save humanity's future in space!

>>> OBJECTIVE <<<

  • MELT space debris using your plasma torch before it falls to Earth
  • AVOID colliding with large asteroids and debris
  • PROTECT the International Space Station from impacts
  • SCORE points by successfully melting junk into harmless clumps

>>> CONTROLS <<<

DESKTOP (Mouse/Keyboard):

  • MOUSE MOVEMENT - Aim satellite direction
  • LEFT CLICK - Fire plasma torch
  • RIGHT CLICK / UP ARROW - Thrust forward
  • M KEY - Toggle background music
  • P KEY - Take screenshot
  • ESC KEY - Return to games menu

MOBILE (Touch):

  • LEFT JOYSTICK - Control satellite direction
  • TORCH BUTTON - Fire plasma torch
  • THRUST BUTTON - Accelerate forward

GAMEPAD:

  • LEFT STICK - Rotate satellite
  • RIGHT TRIGGER - Fire plasma torch
  • LEFT TRIGGER / A BUTTON - Thrust

>>> STRATEGY TIPS <<<

  • Keep your plasma torch aimed at debris for continuous heating - debris must reach melting point before it breaks apart
  • Smaller debris melts faster - prioritize large pieces that pose the biggest threat
  • Watch for debris heading toward the ISS - protecting it earns bonus points
  • Use thrust sparingly - momentum carries you in space, and fuel is limited
  • Melted clumps fall safely to Earth - don't waste torch energy on already-melting debris

>>> SCORING <<<

  • SMALL DEBRIS: 100 points
  • MEDIUM DEBRIS: 250 points
  • LARGE DEBRIS: 500 points
  • ISS PROTECTION BONUS: 1000 points
